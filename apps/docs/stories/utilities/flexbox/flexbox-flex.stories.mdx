<Lead>A shorthand property for setting `flex-grow`, `flex-shrink`, and `flex-basis` simultaneously.</Lead>

## Classes

<CssFlexboxFlex />

## Initial

The flex-initial setting configures `flex-grow` to 0, `flex-shrink` to 1, and `flex-basis` to auto, which is the default browser behavior.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="flex gap-normal mb-normal">
    <div class="flex-initial bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Hello</div>
    <div class="flex-initial bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Hello World</div>
    <div class="flex-initial bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Flex 1

Establishes both `grow` and `shrink` factors to 1, enabling automatic expansion and contraction, while maintaining a `flex-basis` of 0.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="flex gap-normal mb-normal">
    <div class="bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Hello</div>
    <div class="bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Hello World</div>
    <div class="bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</div>
</div>
<div class="flex gap-normal">
    <div class="flex-1 bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Hello</div>
    <div class="flex-1 bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Hello World</div>
    <div class="flex-1 bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Auto

By setting both `grow` and `shrink` factors to 1, the item will automatically expand and contract, while maintaining a `flex-basis` of auto.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
    <div class="flex gap-normal mb-normal">
        <div class="bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Hello</div>
        <div class="bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Hello World</div>
        <div class="bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</div>
    </div>
    <div class="flex gap-normal">
        <div class="flex-auto bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Hello</div>
        <div class="flex-auto bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Hello World</div>
        <div class="flex-auto bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</div>
    </div>
`}
/>

## None

Equivalent to `0 0 auto`, indicating that the element neither expands nor contracts.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="flex gap-normal mb-normal">
  <div class="flex-1 bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</div>
  <div class="flex-none bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</div>
  <div class="flex-1 bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Responsive

Tailored responsive options are at your disposal for customizations according to screen size.
Simply append a responsive breakpoint keyword with a dash, like `tablet:flex-1`, to apply the corresponding responsive class.
