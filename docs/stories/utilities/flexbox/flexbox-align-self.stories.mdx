## Classes

<CssFlexboxAlignSelf />

## Start

Elements are positioned at the beginning of the container.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="flex align-items-stretch flex-wrap gap-normal" style="min-height: 200px">
    <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 50px">1</div>
    <div class="flex align-self-start align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 50px">2</div>
    <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 50px">3</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Center

Elements are centered within the container.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="flex align-items-stretch flex-wrap gap-normal" style="min-height: 200px">
    <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 50px">1</div>
    <div class="flex align-self-center align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 50px">2</div>
    <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 50px">3</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## End

Elements are aligned to the end of the container.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="flex align-items-stretch flex-wrap gap-normal" style="min-height: 200px">
    <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 50px">1</div>
    <div class="flex align-self-end align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 50px">2</div>
    <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 50px">3</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Stretch

The elements are expanded to fill the container.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="flex align-items-stretch flex-wrap gap-normal" style="min-height: 200px">
    <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; height: 50px">1</div>
    <div class="flex align-self-stretch align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 50px">2</div>
    <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; height: 50px">3</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Baseline

Elements are aligned with the baseline of the container.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="flex align-items-stretch flex-wrap gap-normal" style="min-height: 200px">
    <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 50px">1</div>
    <div class="flex align-self-baseline align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 50px">2</div>
    <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 50px">3</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Responsive

Tailored responsive options are at your disposal for customizations according to screen size.
Simply append a responsive breakpoint keyword with a dash, like `tablet:align-self-start`, to apply the corresponding responsive class.
