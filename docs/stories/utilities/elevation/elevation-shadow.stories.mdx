## Classes

<CssElevationShadow />

## Box Shadow

The default shadow for cards is the normal one. Reserve the large shadow, for instance, for hover effects.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center gap-xx-large">
    <div class="shadow-normal flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal p-normal">shadow-normal</div>
    <div class="shadow-large flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green radius-normal p-normal">shadow-large</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Text Shadow

Text with a light shadow to increase readability on images.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<p class="text-shadow">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consetetur sadipscing elitr, sed diam nonumy eirmod tempor invidunt ut labore et dolore magna aliquyam erat, sed diam voluptua.</p>
`}
/>

## Responsive

Tailored responsive options are at your disposal for customizations according to screen size.
Simply append a responsive breakpoint keyword with a dash, like `tablet:shadow-normal`, to apply the corresponding responsive class.

<CssResponsiveTable />

## Pseudo States

States such as Focus, Hover, and Active can be utilized by applying the respective prefixes: **focus:**, **hover:**, and **active:**.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="bg-green-3 hover:shadow-normal active:shadow-large cursor-pointer">
  <p class="p-normal">Hover and click me!</p>
</div>
`}
/>
