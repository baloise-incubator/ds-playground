## Classes

## Wrap

Flexible items will wrap if needed.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="flex flex-wrap" style="max-width: 500px">
  <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green m-normal radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">1</div>
  <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green m-normal radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">2</div>
  <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green m-normal radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">3</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Wrap-Reverse

Flexible items will wrap if needed, but in reverse order.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="flex flex-wrap-reverse" style="max-width: 500px">
  <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green m-normal radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">1</div>
  <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green m-normal radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">2</div>
  <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green m-normal radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">3</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Nowrap

Flexible items maintain their position without wrapping.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="flex flex-nowrap" style="max-width: 500px">
  <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green m-normal radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">1</div>
  <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green m-normal radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">2</div>
  <div class="flex align-items-center justify-content-center bg-green m-normal radius-normal" style="min-width: 200px; min-height: 100px">3</div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Responsive

Tailored responsive options are at your disposal for customizations according to screen size.
Simply append a responsive breakpoint keyword with a dash, like `tablet:flex-nowrap`, to apply the corresponding responsive class.
