## Responsive

Tailored responsive options are at your disposal for customizations according to screen size.
Simply append a responsive breakpoint keyword with a dash, like `tablet:h-full`, to apply the corresponding responsive class.

<CssResponsiveTable />

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="flex flex-wrap align-items-center justify-content-center">
  <div class="radius-normal bg-green-2 w-16rem h-8rem p-small m-small">
    <div class="h-full tablet:h-auto radius-normal bg-green-4 p-small flex align-items-center justify-content-center">h-full on small screen</div>
  </div>
</div>
`}
/>
