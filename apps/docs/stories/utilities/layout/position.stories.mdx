## Static

An element with a static position is rendered in accordance with the natural flow of the page and remains unaffected by adjustments made through the top, left, right, and bottom properties.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="relative">
  <div class="static bg-green-2 p-normal radius-normal" style="min-width: 300px; min-height: 120px;">
    <p>Static</p>
    <div class="absolute bottom-none left-0 bg-green radius-normal p-normal" style="min-width: 100px; min-height: 56px">
      Absolute
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
`}
/>

## Relative

An element with a relative position is presented relative to its initial placement. Utilizing the top, left, right, and bottom properties allows for adjustments to the element's position.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="relative">
  <div class="relative bg-green-2 p-normal radius-normal" style="min-width: 300px; min-height: 140px;">Relative
    <div class="absolute bottom-none left-0 bg-green p-normal radius-normal" style="min-width: 100px; min-height: 56px">
      Absolute
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

`}
/>

## Absolute

A fixed element is placed in relation to the viewport and remains consistently positioned, unaffected by scrolling actions on the page.

<Code
  language="html"
  code={`
<div class="mb-normal">
  <div class="relative bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">
    <p class="mt-none">Relative</p>
    <div class="static bg-green-3 radius-normal p-normal" style="min-width: 300px; min-height: 150px;">
      <p class="mt-none">Static</p>
      <div class="static bottom-none left-0 bg-green-4 radius-normal p-normal" style="min-width: 120px; min-height: 56px">
        Static
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="relative bg-green-2 radius-normal p-normal">
  <p class="mt-0">Relative</p>
  <div class="static bg-green-3 radius-normal p-normal" style="min-width: 300px; min-height: 150px;">
    <p class="mt-0">Static</p>
    <div class="absolute bottom-none left-0 bg-green-4 radius-normal p-normal" style="min-width: 120px; min-height: 56px">
      Dynamic
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
`} />

## Responsive

Tailored responsive options are at your disposal for customizations according to screen size.
Simply append a responsive breakpoint keyword with a dash, like `tablet:absolute`, to apply the corresponding responsive class.
